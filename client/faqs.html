Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this system?

The system helps students assess their stress levels, identify key stressors, and receive personalized recommendations to manage stress effectively.

Who can use this system?

The system is available to all college students at the University of Cabuyao.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How do I start a stress assessment?

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the "Test Stress" section on your dashboard.
  3. Answer all the questions in the assessment form.
  4. Submit your responses to view your results.

Can I view my stress history?

Yes, you can view your history in the "Historical Data" section, where you’ll find records of previous stress assessments and track your progress over time.

How does the recommendation feature work?

The system provides personalized strategies based on your assessment, such as time management tips, relaxation techniques, and counseling.

Are the recommendations reliable?

Yes, the recommendations are validated by licensed psychometricians and psychologists to ensure accuracy and relevance.

Is my data secure?

We prioritize user privacy and ensure that all data is stored securely with encryption methods. Your information will not be shared with third parties.

What should I do if the system isn’t working?

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Try using a different browser.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies.
