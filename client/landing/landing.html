About Stress Check

Stress Check is a comprehensive tool designed specifically for college students at the University of Cabuyao to assess and manage their stress levels. With personalized recommendations, our platform helps users understand their mental well-being and offers solutions to reduce stress. By leveraging data analysis, we aim to create a stress-free environment for students to thrive academically and personally.


Is this quiz accurate?

This stress level test is not meant to be used as a diagnostic tool.

Based on your answers to the questions, you’ll receive a result that gives you an idea of how stressed you are.

But the results won’t tell you whether your health is at risk from the stress you’re experiencing. While the results can offer some insight, they won’t determine if your stress is impacting your health. If you’re feeling concerned about your stress or its physical effects, it’s important to reach out to a healthcare professional, such as a doctor or mental health specialist, who can offer support and guidance. Your well-being matters.
